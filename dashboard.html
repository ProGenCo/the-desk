INDUSTRY ANALYSIS RESEARCH

How Prop Firms Make Money (300K+ Account Study)

  • Challenge pass rate: 5-10% industry avg (Apex claims 15-20%)
  • Only ~7% of eval purchasers ever receive a payout (FPFX Tech data)
  • ~14% get funded, ~45% of funded traders get at least one payout
  • Average payout: 4% of account size (~$2,000 on a 50k account)
  • Primary revenue: eval fees. A firm with 10k monthly applicants at $150/mo = $1.5M/mo
  • 40%+ of unregulated firms shut down in 2025. 80-100 firms collapsed 2023-2025
  • CFTC considering CTA classification for US prop firms — regulation incoming

What Profitable Prop Traders Do Differently

  • Risk 0.25-1% per trade max — on 50k w/ $2,500 DD = $62-250 risk/trade
  • Secure and stop — hit daily target, close platform. Widely used by winners
  • Stop after 2 consecutive losses — cool-down rule prevents tilt
  • Multi-account fleet — 5-10 accounts across 3-4 firms with copy trading
  • Account triage — cut losing accounts fast, feed winners
  • Rule mastery — screenshot rules at funding date, track changes
  • Treat each day as independent — yesterday's P&L doesn't affect today's risk
  • Never revenge trade. Prop trading is about extraction, not account growth

Multi-Account Fleet Strategy

  • Sweet spot: 5-10 accounts across 3-4 firms
  • Copy trading supported: Apex, MFFU, Phidias, BluSky, TickTick Trader
  • Budget 2 eval replacements/month (~$100-150/mo maintenance cost)
  • At 40% personal pass rate: break-even in ~10 months of eval fees
  • Without copy trading: 3-7 accounts is practical for manual execution

Red Flags & Blacklisted Firms

  • Crypto Fund Trader — backdated rules to deny payouts at scale
  • Fidelcrest — retroactive rule changes, withheld payouts without appeal
  • True Forex Funds — correct trades marked as violations, accounts canceled
  • My Forex Funds — froze payouts, deleted funded accounts
  • Consistency rule trap — 30-50% rules force more trading to dilute big days
  • Hidden fees — activation ($130-360), monthly PA ($85/mo), data ($15-30/mo)
  • Rules tightening industry-wide — ETF cut max accts 20->5, Topstep changed split 100%->90/10

Proven Payers (Total Payouts)

  • Apex: $598M+ paid out since 2022 — $15.4M avg monthly
  • Topstep: Industry pioneer — publicly tracks all payouts
  • Tradeify, MFFU, Take Profit Trader — regularly cited as reliable

How Prop Firm Payouts Are Taxed

CONSISTENCY RULE MATRIX WATCH OUT

Know Before You Trade

  • Tradeify Flex: NONE — best for your style
  • Topstep: 50% — no single day > 50% of total
  • Apex: 30% — best day ≤ 30% of total (P1-5)
  • Bulenox: 40% — moderately restrictive
  • Elite Trader: 40% — includes prior withdrawals (compounds!)
  • Phidias: 30% — standard, manageable

The Consistency Trap

If you have one big day (say $800), and your total is $2,000, that one day = 40%. You MUST keep trading to dilute it below the threshold before withdrawing — risking breach in the process. This is the #1 hidden payout blocker in the industry.

How to Beat It

  • Cap daily gains at 2x your target ($300 instead of $150)
  • Spread profits evenly — $150/day is better than $600 + 4 flat days
  • Track consistency % daily in this system
